The most beautiful places in Borovoye

Mount Okzhetpes

The stone block, located in the middle of Lake Borovoye, consists of three giant stone slabs with rounded edges, which form a pyramidal structure with a height of more than 300 meters.

Zhumbaktas Rock

Those who are interested in the village of Borovoe in Kazakhstan, photos of the city and its natural attractions, should get acquainted with another amazing natural phenomenon – the 18-meter rock Zhumbaktas. It is located near the mountain Okzhetpes. The name means "Mystery Rock".

Kenesary Cave

In Burabay district, not far from the sanatorium "Okzhetpes", in a pine forest there is a cave named after the last Kazakh khan, the leader of the liberation struggle against tsarist Russia – Kenesary Kasymov.
